Technical Questions

  1. What brush types are supported?

    Our service is designed exclusively for Procreate stamp brushes. It is not designed for dynamic painting, texture, or pattern brushes. Our tool extracts the core stamp image at its native resolution. For best results, the original stamp image should be 1024x1024px or larger.

  2. What is the maximum file size?

    Each .brushset file can be up to 50MB. For sets larger than this, we recommend splitting them into smaller sets within Procreate before uploading to ensure a fast and reliable conversion.

  3. Are the converted PNG files transparent?

    Currently, all converted PNGs are exported with a solid white background to ensure maximum compatibility and processing speed. The white background can be easily removed in any standard design software.

  4. Do I need an iPad or Mac to use this service?

    Not at all! Our converter is a web-based tool that works in any modern browser on any device, including Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS.

File & Download Questions

  1. How do I find my `.brushset` files on my iPad?

    You first need to export your brushes from Procreate to the Files app.

    • In Procreate: Open the Brush Library, swipe left on the brush set, and tap "Share".
    • Save to Files: In the share menu, choose "Save to Files". We recommend creating a folder named "Brush Exports" to keep things organized.
  2. Where do my downloaded files go?

    After conversion, your `.zip` file is downloaded directly through your browser. You can find it in your browser's "Downloads" list or in your computer's "Downloads" folder. On mobile, check your "Files" app.

  3. Can I convert brushes from Procreate Dreams?

    Our converter is compatible with standard `.brushset` files from Procreate 5 and newer. At this time, it does not support files from Procreate Dreams or very old, legacy Procreate versions due to different file structures.
